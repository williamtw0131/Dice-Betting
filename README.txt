This is a little game allow you to bet on dice.

You will start at $500, and you can bet big, small, or even.

The result will only be win or lose.

And if you win, you will get the same amount of your bet.

And if you lose, you will lose the money you bet.

You can either quit or play untill the game end, which is when your money become none.

that's all.
